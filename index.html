About me

I'm Fangqiao Hu (胡芳侨), a second year PhD candidate advised by Prof. Hui Li in Center of Structural Monitoring and Control, Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). I got my Bachelor's degree from HIT, too.

My research topics are 3D computer vision & machine learning and their applications in structural health monitoring & civil engineering, for example, 3D reconstruction for civil structures to enable remote visual inspection.

Publications

Learning Structural Graph Layouts and 3D Shapes for Long Span Bridges 3D Reconstruction

Fangqiao Hu, Jin Zhao, Yong Huang, and Hui Li*

A deep learning-based 3D reconstruction method for long-span bridges is proposed in this paper, where multi-view images and point cloud are encoded into a hybrid global feature, then decoded into a binary tree to model the high-level structural relation graph, and low-level 3D geometric shapes to model the geometric properties.
